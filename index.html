About Me

I manage enterprise IT infrastructure across multiple brand entities, overseeing Microsoft 365, SharePoint, Intune, Entra ID, Exchange Online, AWS, Route 53, and 3CX Phone System. My work spans device compliance, identity governance, DNS migrations, SSL provisioning, and branch-level support, backed by a strong discipline around documentation, SOPs, and knowledge management.

My skill set sits across infrastructure (cloud, endpoint management, identity, telephony), development (Python, Django, Flutter, REST APIs), and AI (LLMs, prompt engineering, agentic systems, Copilot integrations), and all delivered with a structured project coordination mindset through Jira-based service management.
